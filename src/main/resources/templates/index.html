About us

What is Snooker House?

Snooker House is an online platform that allows the perfect place to cool off on a hot summer day. The crystal-clear water is refreshing and invigorating, and the sun-drenched deck is the perfect place to relax. Come and swim with us today!. .

Looking for a place to relax and have fun? Look no further than our pool. With a poolside bar and grill, you can enjoy a refreshing drink and a delicious meal while you swim. So come on down and let us make your summer unforgettable.

What we provide?

Booking a snooker has never been easier. with our booking service! Our platform allows you to quickly and easily book a snooker at your preferred location, date, and time with just a few clicks. Our real-time booking system ensures that you get the most updated and accurate information on availability, so you can easily find and book that suits your schedule and needs.

Looking for a snooker to swim with your friends or team? Our pool availability service has got you covered! With just a few clicks, you can easily search and find the best snooker near you based on your preferred location, date, and time. Our real-time pool availability feature ensures that you get the most updated and accurate information on available pools, allowing you to easily book the pool that suits your schedule and needs.

Our customer support team is always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have, and we encourage you to reach out to us if you need any assistance or have any feedback about our services.
